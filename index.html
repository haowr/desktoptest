WHAT IS BIG BEANS?

A revolutionary gaming token with an innovative buy-back system on BSC

Big Beans is a gaming token focused on integrating the blockchain with gaming, introducing an innovative way to earn while playing. The Beans Meta will be the virtual world where the Beans ecosystem will exist; it will have virtual land, NFTs, P2E, gaming and cartoons.

Big Beans will be one of the first NFT driven P2E gaming platforms on the Binance Smart Chain. Big Beans is a multiplayer role-playing game centred around the Beans NFT collection. In the game, players farm, compete and breed with one another to earn $BEANS tokens.
